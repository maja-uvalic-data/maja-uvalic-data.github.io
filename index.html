About Me

I am a licensed pharmacist with several years of experience in community pharmacy in Serbia and Germany. Through my work, I discovered a strong interest in data, process optimization, and evidence-based decision making.

To build a new career path, I completed a Data Analytics program where I gained hands-on experience with Python, SQL, Tableau, and Excel. My main focus is on exploring real-world datasets, cleaning and transforming data, and presenting results in a way that is easy to understand for different stakeholders.

I am particularly interested in roles at the intersection of healthcare, pharma, and data analysis, where I can use both my domain expertise and my analytical toolkit.
