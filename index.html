About me

I’m Maja Uvalić, a Data Analyst with a PhD in Pharmacy, based in Germany. I combine analytical thinking with my pharmaceutical background to explore complex datasets and turn them into clear, actionable insights that support decision-making, process improvement, and strategy.

To build a new career path, I completed a Data Analytics program where I gained hands-on experience with Python, SQL, Tableau, and Excel. My main focus is on exploring real-world datasets, cleaning and transforming data, and presenting results in a way that is easy to understand for different stakeholders.

I am particularly interested in roles at the intersection of healthcare, pharma, and data analysis, where I can use both my domain expertise and my analytical toolkit.

Main Projects

Below you can find a selection of case studies from my data analytics training. Each project covers the full workflow from problem definition to insights and recommendations.

Instacart Basket Analysis

Customer segmentation and product pattern analysis for an online grocery retailer. The goal was to help Instacart understand different customer groups, ordering habits, and product affinities to support targeted marketing strategies.

Tools: Python, pandas, seaborn, Excel, Tableau

Rockbuster Stealth Data Strategy

SQL-based analysis for a movie rental company expanding into online streaming. I explored customer behavior, revenue by region, and subscription patterns to inform Rockbuster’s market entry strategy and identify high-value customer segments.

Tools: PostgreSQL, SQL, Tableau, PowerPoint

  • View presentation

Preparing for Influenza Season

Exploring weather and influenza-related patterns to support better planning for the flu season. This project combines domain knowledge from healthcare with data analysis to help allocate medical staff and resources more effectively.

Tools: Python, APIs, pandas, visualization libraries
