Project Overview

Rockbuster Stealth LLC is a global movie rental company that previously operated physical stores worldwide. With the rise of streaming platforms like Netflix and Amazon Prime, the company faced increasing competition. To remain competitive, Rockbuster plans to use its existing movie licenses and launch a new online video rental service. This data analysis aims to provide insights into customer behavior to help management develop an effective strategy for the upcoming launch.

Key Questions

  • Which movies contributed the most/least to revenue gain??
  • What was the average rental duration for all videos?
  • Which countries are Rockbustercustomers based in?
  • Where are customers with a high lifetime value based?
  • Do sales figures vary between geographic regions?

Data & Tools

The Rockbuster dataset is a relational SQL database containing tables on film inventory, customers, payments, staff, stores, and rental history. I used the following tools for analysis and insights:

  • PostgreSQL – querying, joins, CTEs, aggregations
  • pgAdmin 4 – database management and data exploration
  • Tableau Public – visualization and insights presentation

Methodology

  1. Reviewed database schema and identified primary/foreign key relationships
  2. Cleaned and standardized inconsistent values to ensure data accuracy
  3. Merged tables into a single analysis-ready dataframe
  4. Joined tables and filtered relevant fields for analysis
  5. Performed aggregations to examine customer behavior, rental trends, and film performance
  6. Visualized results using Tableau dashboards to support strategic decision-making for a streaming transition

Key Insights

  • Rockbuster customers most enjoyed Telgraph Voyage, Zorro Ark, and Wife Turn.
  • Most popular films are rated PG-13. Leading genres include Sports, Science-Fiction, and Animation.
  • India, China, United States, Japan, and Mexico generate the highest film revenues.
    • The most valuable customers are located in Mexico, Turkey, the United States, and India.

Recommendations

  • Focus on Sports, Sci-Fi and Animations Films. Promote these genres to maximize engagement and rentals.
  • Implement marketing strategies specifically for India, China, the US, Japan, and Mexico to drive further growth.
  • Implement loyalty programs or personalized offers for customers in Mexico, Turkey, US, and India to retain high-value clients.
